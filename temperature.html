Max Temperature Scatter Plots

As expected, maximum temperature at a city correlated strongly with the city's latitude. Temperatures were somewhat higher in the Southern hemisphere, perhaps due in part to the lower landmass of the Southern hemisphere leading to fewer cities at very low latitudes.
