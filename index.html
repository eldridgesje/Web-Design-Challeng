Summary: Latitude Scatter Plots.

The purpose of this assignment was to analyze the relationship between a city's latitude and its weather. We pulled a list of hundreds of cities randomly from a geographical API. We then used a weather API to pull a variety of weather data for each city, including temperature, humidity, cloudiness, and wind speed.

We created a scatter plot for each kind of weather data. We discovered that while temperature was strongly correlated with latitude, none of the other kinds of data displayed a clear correlation. We concluded that latitude has a strong impact on temperature, but little or no influence on humidity, cloudiness, or wind speed.
